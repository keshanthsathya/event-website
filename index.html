Buzz early, answer within 3 seconds. Wrong = -2 points.

No pointing, signaling, or non-verbal hints. Warning. Repeat = -1 point.

3. Mystic Swap Rule

Teams can swap ONE question with another team per round. Only the difficulty level is shown, not the question.
