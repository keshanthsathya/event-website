Rules

Please read the rules carefully

  • Each team should have at least 2 and preferably 3 members; there is no strict upper limit on total teams.
  • All teams must register using the official form before the start of the event.
  • Be respectful and follow the judges' instructions; decisions by the judges are final.
  • Any form of cheating or disruptive behavior will lead to disqualification.
