Organised by the Student Council — A one-day competitive event for all departments. Date 29 Nov 2025 • Venue College Auditorium

About the Event Fast, fun, fair

InterDept Challenge brings teams from every department to compete in quick-fire technical and creative tasks. Events include: Quiz Sprint, Circuit Build, Idea Pitch, and Rapid Prototyping.

RulesKeep it fair
  • Team size: 3–5 participants

  • Bring valid college ID for verification
  • Decisions by judges are final
Event DetailsQuick references

Quiz Sprint

Rapid rounds, buzzer-style, mixed general and technical questions. Team reps only on stage.

Circuit Build

Given components, assemble a working circuit to meet an objective within 45 minutes.

Idea Pitch

Two-minute pitch followed by one-minute Q&A. Presentations only, no live demos.
